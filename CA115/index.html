What is ScaleF?

ScaleF is an informative app and hardware combination that tells you when your fridge is running low on food. The goal of this app is not only to help people to budget properly, but to save people multiple trips to the shops and give them a strong insight into their food storage in the fridge.


The great thing about ScaleF is that we are bridging a gap in the smart fridge market by introducing an affordable alternative. The main competitors in this particular market are LG and Samsung, however these companies are selling their smart fridges for roughly €3000. We at ScaleF believe that the entry price to this market is far too high for the average consumer. Because of this, we have created an affordable alternative to the "traditional" smart fridge.

Features

Mobile Application

With ScaleF, our plan was to make it accessible to everyone to connect with their smart appliance in their home. In order to be able to do this, we developed an application that works seamlessly with our product, just download it from your app store!

Hardware

ScaleF is a replacement for the shelves of your fridge which incorporates a weighing scale. You can modify what ScaleF is set to detect, and what the maximun weight of something should be.

For example, if there is a scale named "Milk", the user can tell the app that they would always like 2 litres of milk in their fridge, once it goes below that weight, the app sends a notification and also displays a warning in the app
