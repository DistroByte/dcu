Gaming Industry Worth

From the small arcade that first started with the small space invaders game to the multi-billion dollar giant of an industry the gaming sector is, let's take a look at the figures.

Current Estimates for 2020

According to We PC, the gaming market could be worth over $90 billion by the end of 2020. That figure includes consoles, computers, and mobiles, with the figure expected to grow by 2.3% annually.

Mobile Games account for nearly 60% of that share, which is in no small part due to the accessibility of mobile games. They don't require dedicated PCs or consoles, hence they can be played anywhere, they also usually don't need much graphical power and thus almost every phone could run them.

Online Content

With the ever growing audience on streaming platforms like Twitch, live reactions to games draw in hundreds of thousands of viewers, promoting a certain game more and more until it becomes as popular as games like Minecraft and Tetris.

These streams bring in revenue through advertisements and "donations" which incentivise the individual to keep playing the game. The same rules apply to sites like YouTube also. ESports is a branch of that and is expected to bring in between $1.1 and $1.3 billion in revenue in 2020
