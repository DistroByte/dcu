Profession

Gaming has become so popular that people are able to just game for a living and make enough money to live and eat off of. An example of this a youtuber, they make videos of themselves playing a game and off that video they get views and from those views they get money, another added bonus can be if a sponsor approaches them and give them a sponser. They can make more money with this sponsor and all they have to do is give them a shoutout in their video

Streamers

Streaming has allowed many video game fans to make a living outside of the traditional 9 to 5 job. There are many video game lovers who make a living by recording Let’s Play videos on video games on YouTube and many of these Let’s Players eventually turn to live streaming where they stream themselves playing games several times a week. Popular streamers can make big money while streaming on platforms like Twitch and as people subscribe to their channel for 5 euro or give a donation of any amount. Streaming has become a very big thing recently, to the point where these streaming platforms are now signing big personalities to just soly stream on their platform. As well streaming allows the content creator to interact with their viewers live giving more to the enjoyment of streaming for both the viewer and the streamer.
