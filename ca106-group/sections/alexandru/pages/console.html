A Game Console: The Gameboy

Created in 1989 this in my opinion is where Nintendo shined the most of all time. It was where toys and computers got combined together.

Although the Gameboy was big and couldn’t fit in your pocket. It was a major hit as Gunpei Yokoi wanted it to be able to last an entire day of use and last the test of time thus explaining the use of such materials like hard plastic making it stronger. The use of game cartridges was where it would shine the most and I would argue were the first to make it the best. To add the build quality of the gameboy really is strong, and so strong that it lasted an explosion that is still fucntioning to this day.

What I love about Nintendo is that they don’t need to use the most advanced technology in their products it just works and provides you with entertainment. The Gameboy and Gameboy Colour (which, as the name implies, had a colour screen vs the normal green toned LCD) sold 118.9 million units worldwide.
