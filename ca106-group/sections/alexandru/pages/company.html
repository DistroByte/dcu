A VideoGame Company : Nintendo

There are 3 notable game companies that stand out in todays age its:

Playstation, Nintendo and Xbox

Although they dominate the video game market I feel that Nintendo are the best at it. Eventhough Playstation and Xbox focus on releasing the best hardware and the best graphics, Nintendo makes do with alright hardware and makes up for it with their games that have always impressed us. From Mario to The Legend of Zelda their stories are what makes them the best.

Nintendo now one of the biggest videogame companies in the world. Has been creating memories for many generations. For example, I used to play the Nintendo Wii and Nintendo DS when I was younger and now my brother plays the Nintendo Switch. The reason I love this company so much is thanks to their humble beginning.

They originally began making beautifully crafted playing cards which were known as Hanafuda. All being created by the one person Fusajiro Yamauchi. His playing cards had gotten so popular that he would soon need to hire staff to aid him with his company, The Nintendo Company. These cards were well-known all-around Japan and very well made. However, were known for some other reasons. Eventually the Hanafuda meaning flower cards got tied into gambling and gangsters such as the “Yakuza”. Thanks to this, people stopped playing Hanafuda as it was tied to gambling and crime. Even though this was a blow to the company the success that these cards brought to Nintendo let it continue to thrive.

Where a new leader was appointed called Hiroshi Yamauchi. He was the one that brought Nintendo to a new frontier, Video Gaming. Although he suffered backlash by his employees and having his authority questioned by many, while also failing on different projects and business ideas such as love Hotels and instant rice etc. Almost bringing the company to the brink of bankruptcy. But all that changed when he brought Nintendo’s focus into toy making.

This was thanks to Gunpei Yokoi who was seen by Hiroshi Yamauchi playing with his little invention a claw game. Hiroshi Yamauchi appointed him as the lead inventor for innovative products for Nintendo and it continued to thrive. Gunpei Yokoi would also be the one to shift Nintendo’s focus to video gaming. Where he saw another passenger on a bullet train going to work playing with his lcd calculator which gave Gunpei Yoko the idea to start creating the “Game and Watch” and starting the family of Nintendo’s Pocket Consoles such as the Gameboy.
