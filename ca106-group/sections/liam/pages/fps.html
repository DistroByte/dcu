First-Person Shooter

What is a First-Person Shooter?

The name itself gives us a self-explanatory view. First-Person Shooter Games, commonly commonly known as FPS Games, are a genre of gaming where the objective of the game is to shoot at specific targets whilst playing through the eyes of the character.

Attributes of Battle Royale Games

In First-Person Shooter Games, the player's character cannot be seen throughout gameplay. Interactions with the character, other characters, players and environment are taken from a personal point of view.

Third Person Shooter - A Misunderstanding

It's important to find the differance between an FPS and a TPS. This is even more necessary now than it was 5 years ago. This is because of the high popularity and status, that the game Fortnite has brought to the gaming scene, especially to the multiplayer gaming scene. Fornite is a third-person shooter and a battle royale game. The way you can tell that Fortnite is a third-person shooter is that it has all the characteristics of a first-person shooter, except that you can see your own character, instead of yourself seeing it through the character's point of view.
