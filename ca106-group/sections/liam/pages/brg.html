Battle Royale Gaming

What is Battle Royale Gaming?

Battle Royale Gaming is a genre that revolves around a game that introduces a competition between competitors until one last person or team is standing.

Attributes of Battle Royale Games

In Battle Royale Games, you'll usually find yourself acquiring resources in order to gain the upper-hand against other opponents. The stronger you are, the more likely your odds of survival are. Of course, there are some exceptions, that you will see in the example section. Finally, a battle-royale game reveals it's true essence through competition involving a large community of players. The most popular battle-royale games today stick to 100 players per game competing against one another. Of course, this number varies, depending on the "match mode" and of course, the popularity of the game.

The Rise of Popular Battle Royale Games

Battle Royale Games are a recent trend. There origins are debatable as they usually came from mods that would have to be installed into the original game. One popular battle royale game that was popular and is debatably popular to this day is the game mode in Minecraft called Hunger Games. This game mode was introduced in 2012. This game mode was inspired from the novel and movie, Hunger Games.

As time passed, were able to see more and more popular games. Starting with the rise of H1Z1, to Player Unknown Battlegrounds and then finally, one of the most popular battle royale game to date, Fortnite. Fortnite changed the game as it brought in a building mechanic. This mechanic brought in more opportunities in battle royale games.

A Different Battle Royale

Fall Guys differs from the typical Battle Royale Games. The game doesn't require the need to collect resources, like the way Fornite, PUBG and Warzone do. The game requires you to complete 5 rounds, each round getting rid of a number of players and the final round crowning the winner.
