Multiplayer Online Battle Arena

What is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena?

This is one of the most popular genres for esports, along with First-Person Shooter Games. Also known as MOBAs, they take on characteristics from other genres such as real-time strategy games and Massively Multiplayer Online (MMO) games. Where they differ though is the format they are played in. MOBAs are mainly focused around competitive play. Games are usually long in terms of time, due to the objective of the game being long-stretched. Players compete against each until they reach this objective and win.

Attributes of MOBAs

MOBAs are objective focused and team-based. Teams will face off against one another in hopes to reach their objective before their oppenent does.

Popular Early MOBA Games

Starcraft, Aeon of Strife is one of the most early MOBA games. It involved defence and attack strategies that we see in today's Mulitplayer Online Battle Arena Games we see today.

Examples of a MOBA Game
